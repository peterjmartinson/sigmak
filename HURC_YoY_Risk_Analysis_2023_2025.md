This report analyzes Item 1A (Risk Factors) disclosures from SEC 10-K filings using proprietary natural language processing and semantic analysis. Each risk factor is:

1. **Extracted** from the company's official SEC filing
2. **Scored** for severity (potential business impact) and novelty (uniqueness vs. historical patterns)
